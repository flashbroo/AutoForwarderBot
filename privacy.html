Privacy Policy

AutoForwarderBot is a subscription-based Telegram automation service that helps users automatically forward messages between their own Telegram chats, groups, and channels. We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling data responsibly.

Information We Collect

  • Telegram User ID
  • Telegram username (if available)
  • Source and destination chat or channel identifiers
  • Subscription and payment status

We do not collect passwords, message content, or sensitive personal information.

Use of Information

Collected information is used strictly for:

  • Providing automated message forwarding services
  • Managing subscriptions and service access
  • Customer support and troubleshooting

Payments

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. AutoForwarderBot does not store or process card details, UPI information, or banking credentials on its servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties except where required by law or for payment processing via Razorpay.

Data Retention & Deletion

User data is retained only while the subscription is active. Users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
