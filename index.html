AutoForwarderBot

Telegram Channel Automation Service

AutoForwarderBot is a Telegram-based automation tool that helps channel owners automatically forward messages from their own source channels to their own destination channels.

The bot works only with channels where the user has administrator access. All payments are processed securely using Razorpay.

About Us

AutoForwarderBot provides Telegram channel automation services for administrators and content owners.

We do not host, store, or distribute third-party content. Users are responsible for ensuring they have permission to forward content from connected channels.

Services

Our subscription-based services include:

  • Automatic message forwarding
  • Scheduled forwarding
  • Multiple channel management
  • Premium automation features
  • Customer support

All services are delivered digitally.

How It Works

1. User starts AutoForwarderBot on Telegram
2. User connects their own source and destination channels
3. User selects a subscription plan
4. Messages are forwarded automatically based on settings
