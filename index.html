AutoForwarderBot

Telegram Channel Automation Service

AutoForwarderBot is a Telegram-based automation tool that helps channel owners automatically forward messages from their own source channels to their own destination channels.

The bot works only with channels where the user has administrator access. Payments are processed securely via authorized payment gateways.

About Us

AutoForwarderBot provides automation tools for Telegram channel administrators and content owners.

We do not host, store, or distribute third-party content. All forwarding actions are initiated and controlled by the user.

Services

  • Automatic message forwarding
  • Multiple channel management
  • Subscription-based access
  • Premium automation features
  • Customer support

How It Works

1. Start AutoForwarderBot on Telegram
2. Connect your own source and target channels
3. Choose a subscription plan
4. Messages are forwarded automatically

Compliance & Usage Policy

This service is intended for lawful use only.

Users must own or manage all connected channels and comply with Telegram’s Terms of Service and applicable laws.
